What is this?
This is a map application built around the popular podcast Serial.
How do I use it?
This application is split into three interactive components!
  • Timeline - The timeline at the bottom contains time stamped calls and testimonies. You can drag across the day and select an hour at a time to view evidence in the ...
  • Stories Panel - The panel you're reading in right now is the stories panel. Here, pieces of evidence will dynamically populate in these little cards, providing information such as the title, a timestamp and a short description. Clicking on a card will activate an icon on the...
  • Map - The map is probably pretty self explanitory. You can pan with your mouse, zoom in and out with your scroll wheel or with the +/- buttons in the top left. Clicking or hovering on any icons on the map will display a popup with additional information.
